incredients


500 grams boneless chicken, cut into bite-sized pieces
2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
2 tablespoons yogurt
1 tablespoon red chili powder
1/2 tablespoon turmeric powder
1 tablespoon coriander powder
1/2 tablespoon garam masala powder
1 egg
2 tablespoons cornflour
Salt to taste
Oil for deep frying
Few curry leaves
2-3 green chilies, slit
Lemon wedges (for garnish)
Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)

Marinate the Chicken:
In a large bowl, combine the chicken pieces with ginger-garlic paste, yogurt, red chili powder, turmeric powder, coriander powder, garam masala powder, egg, and cornflour.
Mix well to coat the chicken evenly.
Let the chicken marinate for at least 30 minutes (you can also marinate it overnight in the refrigerator for better flavor).

Fry the Chicken:
Heat oil in a deep frying pan or kadai over medium heat.
Once the oil is hot, carefully add the marinated chicken pieces in batches and fry them until they are golden brown and crispy.
This should take about 7-8 minutes per batch.
Remove the fried chicken pieces and place them on a paper towel to drain excess oil.

Tempering:
In another pan, heat a tablespoon of oil over medium heat.
Add curry leaves and slit green chilies. Saut√© for a minute until they release their aroma.

incredients


500 grams chicken drumettes or wingettes
2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
1 teaspoon red chili powder
1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
1 teaspoon garam masala powder
1 tablespoon soy sauce
1 tablespoon cornflour
1 egg
Salt to taste
Oil for deep frying
Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
Lemon wedges for garnish

instruction



Marinate the Chicken:
In a bowl, mix chicken drumettes with ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala powder, soy sauce, cornflour, egg, and salt.
Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.

Fry the Chicken:
Heat oil in a deep frying pan or kadai over medium.
Once the oil is hot, add the marinated chicken pieces in batches and fry until they are golden brown and crispy.
This should take about 5-6 minutes per batch.
Remove the fried chicken and place them on a paper towel to drain excess oil.

Serve:
Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and lemon wedges.
Serve hot with your favorite dipping sauce.
